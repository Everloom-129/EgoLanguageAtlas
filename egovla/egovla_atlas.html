EgoVLA, Yang et al., 2025

EgoVLA is pretrained on egocentric human manipulation videos drawn from HOI4D, HOT3D, HoloAssist and TACO, paired with their language task instructions, learning to predict future wrist poses and MANO hand parameters from images, instructions and proprioception. A unified human and robot action space lets it retarget predicted human actions to a bimanual humanoid via inverse kinematics, then fine-tune on a few robot demonstrations. It reuses the source datasets' labels rather than publishing a new standalone corpus.

04Why this is a descriptive entry

Verified via arXiv 2507.12440, rchalyang.github.io/EgoVLA, and github.com/RchalYang/EgoVLA_Release (MIT).

EgoVLA is a VLA method that reuses task instructions already published by HOI4D, HOT3D, HoloAssist and TACO (each under its own license) and releases code (MIT) and checkpoints. It does not assemble or redistribute a new standalone language corpus, so there is nothing independently clusterable here.
