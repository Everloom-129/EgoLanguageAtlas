EgoVLP, Lin et al., NeurIPS 2022 (derived from Ego4D)

EgoVLP is an egocentric video-language pretraining method built on Ego4D. The authors construct EgoClip, a cleaned and filtered text layer over Ego4D narrations that yields about 3.8 million clip-text pairs with verb and noun tags and timestamps. A dual encoder is pretrained with the EgoNCE objective, a contrastive loss aware of verb-noun semantics and narration synonyms, and validated on EgoMCQ, an inter-video and intra-video multiple-choice benchmark. Because EgoClip is derived from Ego4D narrations and inherits the Ego4D License, the strings are not redistributed in this atlas.

04Why this is a descriptive entry

Verified via arXiv 2206.01670, qhlin.me/EgoVLP, and github.com/showlab/EgoVLP (code MIT). EgoClip text inherits the Ego4D License.

EgoClip is openly downloadable as a CSV, but its text is a filtered layer over Ego4D narrations and inherits the gated Ego4D License, which restricts redistribution, and EgoVLP consumes rather than originates the language. The corpus can be analyzed locally after obtaining Ego4D, so the entry is documented descriptively with links to the source rather than embedding Ego4D-derived strings.
