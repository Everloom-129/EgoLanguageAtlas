Corpora (language supervision sources)

Being-H0 descriptive

A dexterous VLA pretrained from large-scale human hand videos via physical instruction tuning and part-level hand-motion tokenization, building the UniHand corpus.
vla-pretrain hand-pose corpus task-instruction
license: code MIT; full UniHand corpus license not released
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

Ego-Exo4D descriptive

Time-synchronized ego and exo video of skilled activity with atomic action descriptions, narrate-and-act narrations, and expert commentary.
corpus perception-pretrain eval-benchmark atomic-substep clip-narration expert-commentary
license: Ego-Exo4D License (gated; redistribution restricted)
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

Ego4D descriptive

Foundational 3,670 hour worldwide egocentric corpus with millions of timestamped free-form narrations and a multi-task benchmark suite.
corpus perception-pretrain eval-benchmark clip-narration video-summary verb-noun-action
license: Ego4D License (gated; redistribution restricted)
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

EgoDex descriptive

829 hours of Apple Vision Pro egocentric video with native 3D hand pose over 194 tabletop tasks, each with a GPT-generated task description.
corpus hand-pose vla-pretrain eval-benchmark task-instruction
license: CC BY-NC-ND: annotation-text redistribution NOT permitted
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

EgoVerse descriptive

Worldwide human egocentric platform, 1,362 h / ~80k episodes / 1,965 tasks, split into controlled EgoVerse-A and industry-scale EgoVerse-I with manipulation-relevant language.
corpus co-train vla-pretrain task-instruction clip-narration atomic-substep
license: code MIT; dataset/annotation redistribution license not stated
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

EgoVid-5M

5 million egocentric clips curated from Ego4D, each paired with a VLM-authored high-level action description and a long caption, for egocentric video generation.
world-model corpus clip-narration verb-noun-action
4,873,088 annotations · 357 clusters · 0/hr · 0.0 h
license: apache-2.0 (annotations; videos under Ego4D License)
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

EgoVLP (EgoClip / EgoNCE / EgoMCQ) descriptive

A pre-filtered, cleaned text layer over Ego4D: about 3.8M clip-text pairs (EgoClip) plus the EgoNCE objective and the EgoMCQ choice benchmark.
perception-pretrain corpus eval-benchmark clip-narration verb-noun-action
license: code MIT; data inherits Ego4D License
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

EPIC-KITCHENS-100

100 hours of unscripted egocentric kitchen activity, densely annotated as verb plus noun action segments parsed from participant narrations.
corpus eval-benchmark verb-noun-action clip-narration
76,885 annotations · 111 clusters · 875/hr · 100.0 h
license: CC BY-NC 4.0
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

HD-EPIC

41 hours of unscripted egocentric cooking across 9 kitchens, with highly-detailed full-sentence narrations, recipe steps, and a 26,550 question VQA benchmark.
corpus eval-benchmark clip-narration verb-noun-action task-instruction vqa dense-multi-aspect
59,454 annotations · 314 clusters · 1,450/hr · 41.2 h
license: CC BY 4.0
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

MotionTrans descriptive

Transfers motion-level manipulation skills from VR-collected human demonstrations to robots through a human-to-robot transformation pipeline and weighted human-robot co-training.
co-train hand-pose corpus task-instruction
license: code MIT; HF dataset has no license tag
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

OakInk2

Bimanual hand-object manipulation organized as a task hierarchy, with complex-task and primitive-substep language descriptions in English and Chinese.
corpus hand-pose affordance task-instruction atomic-substep
3,833 annotations · 64 clusters · 0/hr · 0.0 h
license: CC BY-SA 4.0
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

OpenEgo descriptive

Consolidates six egocentric corpora (1,107 h, 290 tasks) into a unified 21-joint hand-pose format with intention-aligned, timestamped language primitives.
corpus hand-pose vla-pretrain task-instruction atomic-substep verb-noun-action
license: mixed per-source (MIT code; constituents vary, some CC BY-NC-ND)
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

HAT (Humanoid Policy ~ Human Policy) descriptive

An open egocentric human-demonstration corpus (PH2D) with per-demo task instructions and 3D hand poses, co-trained with a Human Action Transformer via differentiable retargeting.
co-train corpus hand-pose task-instruction
license: MIT (dataset)
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

PhysBrain descriptive

An Egocentric2Embodiment pipeline turning raw human egocentric video into schema-driven, evidence-grounded supervision across seven VQA modes, yielding the ~3M-instance E2E-3M dataset.
dense-relabel vla-pretrain perception-pretrain corpus vqa dense-multi-aspect
license: not stated
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

Dense relabeling

DeMiAn (How to Instruct Your Robot) descriptive

Studies language density as a lever: re-labels segments along four aspects (physical motion, scene, arm pose, reasoning) via Qwen3-VL, with a learned instructor selecting an annotation at deployment.
dense-relabel vla-pretrain world-model dense-multi-aspect task-instruction
license: paper CC BY 4.0; dense annotations not released
atlas EN · 中文 · paper

VLA / policy pretraining

EgoVLA descriptive

Pretrains a VLA on egocentric human videos to predict wrist/hand actions, then retargets via inverse kinematics for a bimanual humanoid, with a new manipulation benchmark.
vla-pretrain hand-pose eval-benchmark task-instruction
license: code MIT; reused labels follow each source dataset license
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

LAPA (Latent Action Pretraining) descriptive

Unsupervised vision-language-action pretraining that learns discrete latent actions between video frames from actionless and human video, then fine-tunes them to real robot actions.
vla-pretrain task-instruction
license: code and weights MIT
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

Human + robot co-training

EgoMimic descriptive

Captures egocentric human data with Aria glasses and co-trains a unified human-plus-robot imitation policy on a kinematically-matched bimanual robot.
co-train hand-pose
license: code MIT; HuggingFace dataset license not stated
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

EMMA descriptive

Scales mobile manipulation by co-training egocentric human full-body motion with static robot data, avoiding mobile teleoperation.
co-train
license: code and data license unspecified
atlas EN · 中文 · paper

Affordance / interaction priors

MAPLE descriptive

Learns vision-encoder features that predict object contact points and grasping hand poses at contact from egocentric images, reused to train dexterous manipulation policies.
perception-pretrain affordance hand-pose
license: no license file in repo (default all rights reserved)
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

VRB (Vision-Robotics Bridge) descriptive

Trains a visual affordance model (contact heatmap plus post-contact wrist trajectory) from human video to drive several robot-learning paradigms.
affordance
license: code MIT
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

ZeroMimic descriptive

Distills image-goal-conditioned manipulation skills (open, close, pour, pick-place, cut, stir) from EPIC-KITCHENS human video for zero-shot robot deployment.
affordance hand-pose
license: arXiv listing CC BY 4.0; code/checkpoint license not separately stated
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

Reward / representation learning

VIP descriptive

Learns a self-supervised goal-conditioned value function from unlabeled Ego4D video, providing dense visual rewards for unseen robot tasks.
reward-repr perception-pretrain
license: code CC BY-NC 4.0
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

Perception pretraining

R3M descriptive

A reusable visual representation pretrained on Ego4D for data-efficient robot manipulation.
perception-pretrain
license: code MIT
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

Hand-pose supervision

EgoZero descriptive

Learns closed-loop manipulation from Project Aria smart-glasses human demonstrations with zero robot data, deployed zero-shot on a Franka gripper.
hand-pose
license: code MIT; paper CC BY 4.0
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

Phantom descriptive

Trains robot manipulation from human-only video by converting each demonstration into robot-compatible observation-action pairs via hand-pose estimation and visual editing.
hand-pose
license: code MIT; paper CC BY 4.0
atlas EN · 中文 · paper · data

Diagnostic long-form video-QA benchmark: 5,031 multiple-choice questions over 3-minute Ego4D clips requiring long temporal certificates.
eval-benchmark vqa
