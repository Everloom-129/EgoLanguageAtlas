EgoMimic, Kareer et al., ICRA 2025

EgoMimic collects egocentric human demonstrations with Project Aria glasses, extracting 3D hand tracking and aligning the human and robot embodiments kinematically. Human hand data and teleoperated bimanual-robot data are treated as one observation and action space and co-trained in a single imitation-learning policy. The released HuggingFace data is hand tracking plus video, not a language-annotation corpus.

04Why this is a descriptive entry

Verified via arXiv 2410.24221, egomimic.github.io, github.com/SimarKareer/EgoMimic, and huggingface.co/datasets/gatech/EgoMimic.

EgoMimic is a co-training method whose released dataset is egocentric video with hand tracking, not language. It reuses no external narration corpus and authors no instruction text, so there is nothing clusterable to download. This atlas records it descriptively.
